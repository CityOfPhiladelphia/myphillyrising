About Philly Rising

The PhillyRising Collaborative is the City of Philadelphiaâ€™s new way of doing business. PhillyRising targets neighborhoods throughout Philadelphia that are plagued by chronic crime and quality of life concerns, and establishes partnerships with community members to address these issues. The PhillyRising Team coordinates the actions of City agencies to help neighbors realize their vision for their community through sustainable, responsive, and cost-effective solutions. Together, residents and the City government can keep Philly rising!

Our Objectives

  • Fight crime and the fear of crime; including terrorism
  • Build sustainable, responsive solutions to the concerns of people living and working in each neighborhood
  • Develop cost-effective methods for improving service delivery to each neighborhood
  • Help those living and working in the PhillyRising neighborhoods to realize their vision for their community
